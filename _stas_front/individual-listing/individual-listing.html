About Elmcroft of Roswell

Elmcroft communities’ goal and mission is to: Enrich the lives of those who live and work with us by responding to their unique needs and universal desire for dignity and respect.

Established in 2002, this Elmcroft location holds an assisted living license to care for 75 residents, 61 in assisted living and 14 in memory care.

Among the beautiful apartments and communal gathering spaces, residents love to come together for socializing and activities. There are multiple spaces for gathering inside and out. There are areas to sit and visit or play cards or billiards and more.

Elmcroft of Roswell is proud of their Signature Programs and the life enrichment they provide for their residents:

Vitality Club develops an individualized wellness and daily activities profile for each resident.

The Port of Call series expands resident’s cultural experiences by having a new ‘destination’ every month to enjoy activities and the cuisine from that area.

The Walking Tall program is helping each resident better understand their fall risk and training to help them with balance to stay safe when moving.

Second Wind Dreams helps resident’s dreams come true by providing opportunities to cross something off a bucket list, have an adventure or visit a favorite place.

Chronicles is a memory care program where the approach to care is specialized, built around each resident’s life history to provide the specially trained caregiver’s the insight to understand, support and encourage.

The dining experience is all about choice. Resident’s preferences are considered as the culinary team meets with them regularly for their valued input and suggestions. Dishes created new or more traditional, are always made from scratch with the freshest ingredients from locally sourced farm to table growers.

Elmcroft of Roswell activities are varied as the residents are engaged and enthusiastic! They enjoy live music, movie nights and to showcase the talent in their community.
